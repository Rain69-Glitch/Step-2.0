WELCOME TO STEP 2.0

Dedicated to environmental conservation and sustainability, we are one of the oldest clubs at MIS. Since our establishment in 2012,
we have empowered over 800 students to actively participate in various eco-friendly events and initiatives.
Led by our esteemed club coordinator, Ms. Neeraj Soni Sharma, our mission is to inspire change, promote scientific knowledge, and foster a greener future.

What We Offer

Around 800 students have actively participated in various events organised by our club.

Clean Campus Campaign

A clean campus campaign is super important for keeping our school environment clean and tidy. It's all about raising awareness and encouraging everyone to do their part in keeping the campus clean. We can organize activities like trash clean-ups, recycling drives, and spreading the word about the importance of picking up after ourselves. By working together, we can create a clean and pleasant environment for everyone to enjoy!

Clean Class Campaign

A clean class campaign focuses on maintaining cleanliness and tidiness within our classrooms. It involves encouraging good habits like keeping desks organized, throwing trash in designated bins, and wiping down surfaces regularly. We can also encourage classmates to take responsibility for their personal spaces and remind them of the importance of cleanliness for a conducive learning environment. Together, we can make our classes clean and comfortable for everyone!

Mensural Hygiene Day

Menstrual Hygiene Day is an important day that helps us learn about taking care of ourselves during our periods. It's all about making sure we have the right products and facilities to stay clean and comfortable. We want to make sure everyone feels supported and not embarrassed about menstruation.
