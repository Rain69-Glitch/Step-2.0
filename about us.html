About us


Step 2.0, one of the most longstanding clubs at our school, It has a fascinating history dating back to its inception in 2012 when it was initially known as the Science-Eco Club.
Later, it was officially renamed "Step 2.0" or the "Save the Earth Program" by the Principal. Ms. Neeraj Soni Sharma has been the dedicated club coordinator,
guiding the club to flourish as a incentive for promoting environmental awareness and sustainability.


Over the years, Step 2.0 has experienced significant growth, attracting an impressive participation of around 800 students in its various organized events.
These activities have played a crucial role in instilling a strong sense of responsibility and eco-consciousness among the student body.
From tree-planting initiatives to recycling campaigns, the club has undertaken numerous projects to address environmental challenges effectively.


Step 2.0 continues to motivate and mobilize students to take tangible steps towards protecting the planet.
It stands as a valuable platform for young minds to collaborate, exchange ideas, and actively contribute to creation of more sustainable future act.
